Pre-conference drinks @ Nieuwmarkt

De Waag on Nieuwmarkt. Photo by wisze

If you’re attending State Of The Map, chances are that you’ll be in Amsterdam by Thursday night. What better way start off your SOTM weekend than to join fellow attendees for some pre-conference drinks? We picked a great place for that: the bustling Nieuwmarkt square (map) in the heart of the old city.

Members of the SOTM organisation team will be at ‘De Waag’ bar / restaurant to meet you. It’s the big building with the towers right in the middle of the square. If the weather is as nice as it has been the last couple of days, we’ll probably be outside ðŸ™‚

If you want to have dinner nearby beforehand, there’s a lot of options. De Waag itself is OK but quite pricey. Excellent and cheap Chinese and Thai food is to be had on Zeedijk, just off the square. Several of the other bars on Nieuwmarkt serve food as well.

See you Thursday from 8pm on!

State of the Map 2009 announced!

The 3rd Annual OpenStreetMap Conference – State Of The Map – will be held in Amsterdam on July 10th, 11th and 12th. The local organising team is very excited to host this wonderful event. If the two previous editions are anything to go by, State Of The Map 2009 is one geo-event you just don’t want to miss!

At the moment, we’re pinning down the important details such as venue, sponsors and of course the programme. All announcements concerning SOTM09 will be made through this website, so check back regularly or add the news feed to your RSS-cruncher of choice.
